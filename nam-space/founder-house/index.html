The Concept

Most retreats give you inspiration and send you home. NāM Founder's House gives you a container to actually build inside. Hosted at Kin Haus on Koh Phangan, it brings together a small, carefully selected group of founders, operators, and creative entrepreneurs — people who are serious about growth and generous with their knowledge.

Week one is fully facilitated — every detail handled, every session held, every meal cooked. What follows is yours: three weeks of self-guided residency inside the same space, same community, same rhythm. Or stay the full month — most people who do say it's where everything clicked. Two modes. One uninterrupted run.

How It Works
01 · The Retreat
Arrive & Go Deep
Fully facilitated from day one. Scooter waiting, chef cooking, massage booked, yoga on. Curated peer sessions, mastermind time, and local immersion built in. Your only job: show up.
02 · The Residency
Stay & Build
Stay for three weeks on your own terms. The space, the people, and the island remain — but the schedule is yours. Work privately, collaborate freely, go deeper. Three weeks is enough to find your rhythm. A month is enough to change something.
03 · The Network
Leave & Stay Connected
The relationships built here don't end when you leave. NāM alumni are a vetted network of operators and builders. Priority access to future retreats, shared deal flow, and an ongoing group that actually knows each other.
