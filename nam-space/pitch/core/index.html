NāM Space

A co-creation and co-living space on Koh Phangan

NāM Space is a workspace and living space for entrepreneurs and creatives who want to do real work in a place that actually supports it.

Focused workspace. Treehouse accommodation. A community that helps you do your best work.

02
The island

Koh Phangan is changing

The island’s image is shifting. It’s no longer just the party and wellness island — it’s becoming a destination for entrepreneurs, creatives, and builders who stay for months, not days.

A new wave

Founders, designers, developers — drawn by the lifestyle, staying for the community. The island is at an inflection point.

AI is accelerating it

Remote-first work keeps growing. AI is enabling smaller teams to build bigger things from anywhere. The talent is coming.

Infrastructure hasn’t caught up

Fast fibre internet is here. International schools, quality healthcare. But there’s no proper workspace built for this audience.

03
The gap

People crave community — and there’s nowhere for it

Workspaces with no soul. Cafés with wifi. Retreats with no work infrastructure. Most spaces offer one thing and bolt the rest on as an afterthought.

There’s a huge lack of spaces that balance work, living, wellness, and real community. No activities, no events, no skill exchange — no place where people come together to work and learn from each other.

The people doing the most meaningful work are making do with places that weren’t designed for how they actually live.

The workspace

Built for focus

Not a café with wifi. A proper workspace designed for how people actually work.

Deep work zone

Quiet, focused area for heads-down work. No calls, no chatter. Just flow.

Communal social area

2 private pods

Napping spaces

Dedicated rest areas. Because the afternoon dip is real and a 20-minute nap fixes everything.

Activity spaces

Multi-use rooms for yoga, workshops, pottery, and classes. Flex space that earns its keep.

07
Philosophy

Structure enables flow

People are craving spaces that provide structure. Not rigidity — rhythm. A space that tells you what belongs here, so you don’t have to figure it all out yourself.

There’s no one way to use NāM. Deep work when you need focus. Social areas when you need energy. Activities when you want to move. Then we wrap programming around it — events, skill shares, communal meals — so the structure carries you forward.

The space does the deciding. You just show up.
