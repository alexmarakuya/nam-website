Corporate Structure

Three paths to structure NāM Space

We are evaluating three corporate structures before committing to investors. All options share the same constants: land stays in Alex's personal name, and investors hold up to 49% of NāM Space Co. Ltd.

Option A

NāM Studio as parent

Studio holds 51%+ of Space. Architecture investment becomes a founding contribution. One new company only.

Recommended near-term
Option B

Parallel companies under Alex

Studio and Space sit side-by-side under Alex personally. Cleanest ring-fencing for investors. No cross-contamination.

Cleanest investor story
Option C

NāM Holdings as group parent

New holding company owns both Studio and Space. Future ventures added as siblings. Most institutional structure.

Most scalable

All options: Land → Alex (personal)  |  Structures → NāM Space Co. Ltd.  |  IP & Brand → NāM Studio Co. Ltd.
