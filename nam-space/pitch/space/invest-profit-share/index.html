Risks

What could go wrong

Seasonality

Koh Phangan has high and low seasons. Diversified revenue (co-working + events + monthly stays) reduces dependence on nightly accommodation.

Regulatory

Thai property and business regulations require careful structuring. We work with established local legal counsel.

Market demand

Remote work is growing but not guaranteed. Our focus on community and quality of space creates defensible demand beyond pure accommodation.

Construction

Building on a tropical island involves weather delays and logistics. Phased development and experienced local contractors mitigate timeline risk.

Occupancy variance

Profit share means returns fluctuate. Conservative scenario (50% occupancy) still yields ~4.1% annualized. Multiple revenue streams provide a floor.
