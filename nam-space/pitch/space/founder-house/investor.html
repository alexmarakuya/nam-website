Available after 5 years with 12 months’ written notice.

Find a buyer. Members vote by simple majority. NāM retains right of first refusal. If no buyer is found within 24 months, NāM buys back at the fixed appreciation price.

Occupancy risk

Built-in demand pipeline

NāM’s 500+ community is an immediate rental audience. Kin Haus hit 70–110% occupancy on opening — demand so strong Alex rented out his own room. Low season is bridged with retreats and startup offsites.

Liquidity risk

Contractual buyback guarantee

NāM guarantees to buy back your unit after 5 years at original price + 5% per year. You are never permanently locked in.

Legal risk

30-year leasehold, full documentation

Three ring-fenced entities: NāM Space Co. Ltd. (master land lease), NāM Founder House Co. Ltd. (SPV — your investment lives here), and NāM Space Management Co. Ltd. (operations). The 3-Rai site is privately owned by Alex Duffner — no landlord dependency.

Construction risk

Phased build, founder on-site

Capital deployed in phases. Alex manages the build on-site with an experienced local team. Kin Haus was built and opened successfully.

Currency risk

EUR & USD payouts on request

Income in THB, but payouts available in EUR or USD at market rate — reducing currency exposure.
