NāM Founder's House

One month with four founders. A facilitated first week, then three weeks to build on your own terms.

July 2026 Koh Phangan, Thailand
You Know This Feeling

You've done the retreats that promise transformation and deliver inspiration at best. You've tried coworking spaces where everyone's optimising alone. You've booked the flights, found the Airbnb, opened the laptop — and felt exactly as scattered as you did at home.

The problem isn't the place. It's the container. Most of them are missing one.

The Concept

NāM Founder's House is a one-month residency for four founders at Kin Haus on Koh Phangan. The first week is fully facilitated — every detail handled, every session curated. The three weeks that follow are yours: self-guided residency inside the same space, same community, same rhythm.

Week one builds the bonds and sets the pace. Weeks two through four let you actually use them. Movement, deep work, community nights, culture days — the weekly structure stays, but the intensity is yours to dial. Most founders say the real breakthroughs happen in week three.

Week one follows this rhythm with full facilitation. Weeks two through four keep the same shape — you choose how much to join.
